Active Alarms

This screen displays all active zone and robot alarms. Active alarms are displayed in color based on level of severity. Operator Stops and System Faults are shown in Red, Warnings are displayed in Yellow, and Status Messages are displayed in Green. For troubleshooting information on a displayed alarm, double click on it.

Note: Changes to this screen can only be made by a user with sufficient access level.

Click the area where you want help.
