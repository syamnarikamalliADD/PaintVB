Intrusion Muting/Skid Gap Setup

The purpose of this screen is to setup the software muting lengths and required Skid Gap for each style of vehicle painted in this booth. The entrance starting mute count is the conveyor counts from the entrance mute limit switch where muting starts. The exit starting mute count is the conveyor counts from the exit mute limit switch where muting starts. The Style Length is the amount of conveyor travel (cm) the photo eyes are muted once muting is started. Therefore, the entrance photo eyes are muted from Entrance Starting Mute Count  to Entrance Starting Mute Count + the Style Length.  The exit photo eyes are muted from Exit Starting Mute Count  to Exit Starting Mute Count + the Style Length. The Skid Gap is the amount of conveyor travel (cm) to hold this Style at the gap stop prior to releasing it to maintain a uniform job gap.

The Intrusion PEC setup and Skid Gap counts are read from and saved to the PLC.

Note: Changes to this screen can only be made by a user with sufficient access level.
