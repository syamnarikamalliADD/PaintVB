Scheduler

This screen is used to setup shift schedules and computer cleanup times. These shift schedules are used by the GUI to log alarms, downtime and production data. PW3 reports can be based on these shift start and end times. The Scheduler will log 3 shifts per day with three breaks in each shift. Shifts and break times can be enabled/disabled depending upon use. (Note: Changes to the scheduler can only be made by a logged in operator with sufficient access level.)

Hold the mouse over the Area where you want help.
