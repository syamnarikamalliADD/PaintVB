PLC I/O Monitor

The PLC I/O screen displays the discrete and analog input/output states (as seen by the PLC) for each PLC I/O rack.  The operator selects the specific PLC I/O rack to view. For discrete signals, GREEN text will be used to indicate the high (1) state, while RED text is used to indicate the low (0) state.  I/O for all PLC racks (including the robot and remote racks if applicable) may be viewed.
